What to Pack for a New Zealand Tour

Clothing

  • Warm layers, such as a fleece or down jacket
  • Waterproof and windproof jacket
  • Hiking boots or sturdy sneakers
  • Sun hat and sunglasses
  • Swimsuit (for hot springs and beaches)

Gear

  • Daypack for hikes and excursions
  • Water bottle and hydration pack
  • Camera and extra batteries
  • Power adapter for New Zealand outlets
  • Binoculars for wildlife viewing

Tour plan

Welcome to Auckland! After checking into your hotel, spend the day exploring the city on your own, or join a guided tour to see the highlights of Auckland.

Today we'll take a tour of Rotorua's geothermal wonders, including bubbling mud pools, geysers, and hot springs. In the evening, enjoy a traditional Maori cultural performance and feast.

Today we'll fly to Queenstown, the adventure capital of New Zealand. Choose from a range of optional activities, including bungy jumping, jet boating, or simply exploring the stunning scenery.

Today we'll take a scenic drive to Milford Sound and enjoy a cruise on the pristine waters of this stunning fjord. Keep an eye out for dolphins, seals, and penguins.
