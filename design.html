Hiking in Fiordland National Park

Explore the stunning Fiordland National Park on foot with our guided hiking tours. Our experienced guides will take you on a memorable journey through valleys, over mountain passes, and beside crystal-clear lakes. With a range of trails to choose from, we have something for everyone, from novice hikers to seasoned trekkers.

Skydiving in Queenstown

Experience the ultimate adrenaline rush with our skydiving tours in Queenstown. Jump from a plane at 15,000 feet and freefall for up to 60 seconds before your parachute opens and you glide back down to earth. With stunning views of the Remarkables mountain range and Lake Wakatipu, this is an experience you'll never forget.
